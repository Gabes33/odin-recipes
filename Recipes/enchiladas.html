Chicken Enchiladas

Description

This traditional Mexican dish is sure to impress everyone at the table. Served in a corn or flour tortilla, the filling consists of chicken, sauce, and veggies.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Cooked Chicken Breast
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 12 oz. Can Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1 Cup Shredded cheese
  • 8 flour or corn tortillas
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 1 6 oz can diced green chiles
  • 1 tbsp. butter
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup chopped scallions

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 °F and grease a large baking dish
  2. Melt butter in saucepan and saute scallions for 3-4 minutes
  3. Add sour cream, condensed soup, and green chiles until combined
  4. Add chicken and half of the cheese and stir until cheese melts
  5. Fill each tortilla with mixture and roll up with seam-side down, then place in baking dish
  6. Repeat until 1/4 of mixture is reserved in pan
  7. Stir milk into reserved mixture, spoon over tortillas, then top with remaining cheese
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden and bubly. Serve
