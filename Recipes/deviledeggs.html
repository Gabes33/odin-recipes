Deviled Eggs

Description

As a classic appetizer that won't last long. Creamy and flavorful is what this dish is all about.

Ingredients

  • 12 Hardboiled Eggs
  • 4 tbsp. Mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp. Maple Sugar
  • 2 tsp. White Vinegar
  • 2 tsp. Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tbsp. Finely Chopped Yellow Onion
  • 1 tbsp. Finely Chopped Celery
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Paprika to taste

Steps

  1. Peel eggs and half eggs lengthwise
  2. Remove Yolks and set whites aside
  3. Combine mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, onion, and celery with mashed yolks. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  4. Stuff mixture into egg whites
  5. Sprinkle with paprika and refridgerate until serving
